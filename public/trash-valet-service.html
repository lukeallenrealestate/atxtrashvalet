How Doorstep Trash Valet Works

Trash valet service is simple: residents place their bagged trash and recycling outside their door by a designated time each evening. Our uniformed crew walks the property, collects from every unit, and transports everything to your dumpster or compactor. By the time residents wake up the next morning, their hallways and breezeways are clean.

For property managers and ownership, the benefits go beyond cleanliness. Valet trash reduces dumpster overflow, eliminates the mess that accumulates around trash rooms and compactor areas, and removes a major source of pest attraction. It's consistently rated as one of the top amenities by apartment residents in Austin — ahead of package lockers and fitness centers in many surveys.

At ATX Trash Valet, we add two things most providers don't: custom schedules designed around your property's specific layout and needs, and daily reports delivered to ownership every morning with full details on the previous night's service. No other valet trash company in Austin offers this level of transparency.

Who Benefits from Trash Valet Service?

Property managers save time and reduce complaints. No more managing trash room cleanliness, fielding resident complaints about dumpster access, or dealing with overflow situations. Valet trash outsources a high-touch, high-frustration task to a dedicated team.

Ownership and asset managers gain visibility through daily reports and can use valet trash as a revenue-generating amenity line item. Many Austin properties bill valet trash as an amenity fee — creating a net-positive revenue stream while improving resident satisfaction.

Residents get a genuinely useful amenity that saves them a nightly trip to the dumpster. In Austin's heat, that alone is worth it. But it also means cleaner hallways, fewer pests, and a property that feels well-managed.
