— About the service

What is valet trash service?

Valet trash service is a doorstep waste collection amenity for apartment communities, condominiums, and other multifamily properties. Instead of asking residents to walk their trash to a dumpster or compactor, our team comes to them. Residents place bagged trash and recycling outside their door at a set time, and our crew collects it on a scheduled route, typically five to seven nights per week.

For Austin property managers, valet trash has shifted from a luxury amenity to a baseline expectation. Top-rated apartment communities across Downtown Austin, South Austin, North Austin, and the suburbs now include doorstep trash service as a core offering. It's consistently ranked among the most-used amenities in resident surveys, ahead of package lockers and community fitness centers in many properties.

The reasons are practical. Doorstep trash valet service eliminates dumpster overflow, removes odor and pest sources from common areas, reduces wear on trash chutes and compactors, and removes a major source of resident complaints. It also creates accountability: with daily reports delivered to ownership every morning, property managers know exactly what happened on their property the night before.

ATX Trash Valet is Austin's locally owned doorstep trash and recycling service, built specifically for the needs of Austin apartment communities. We service properties across the entire Austin metro — from high-rise condos in Downtown Austin to garden-style communities in Round Rock, Cedar Park, and Pflugerville. Every plan is custom-built around your property layout, your residents, and your management goals.

05 — FAQ

Common questions.

What is trash valet service?+
Trash valet service is a doorstep pickup service where our team collects trash and recycling directly from each resident's door on a set schedule. It eliminates the need for residents to walk to dumpsters and keeps common areas clean. It's consistently rated one of the most valued amenities in apartment communities across Austin.
How much does it cost?+
Pricing depends on community size, number of units, and pickup frequency. Every quote is custom-built for your property. Call us at (254) 718-2567 or fill out our form for a free estimate within 24 hours.
What areas do you serve?+
We serve apartment communities across the greater Austin area — Downtown, South Austin, North Austin, East Austin, West Austin, The Domain, Mueller, Riverside, West Campus, Round Rock, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, and surrounding areas.
Do you offer bulk trash pickup?+
Yes. In addition to nightly doorstep valet, we handle bulk trash pickup for move-outs, property cleanups, and oversized items. Schedule one-time or recurring service.
How do daily reports work?+
After every service night, property managers and ownership receive a detailed report — units serviced, issues flagged, contamination notes, and completion status. Delivered automatically every morning.
Can we customize our pickup schedule?+
Absolutely. We build every plan around your property's specific needs — pickup days, times, and special requirements. Whether you need five nights a week or a custom rotation, we design a schedule that works.
How quickly can service start?+
In most cases, we can begin service within one week of signing. We'll walk your property, finalize the schedule, provide resident communication materials, and begin nightly pickup. For larger properties, we may need a few extra days for setup.
What happens during holidays?+
We operate through most holidays. If a service adjustment is needed, we communicate proactively with your management team well in advance to arrange coverage or make-up service. Your residents won't be left wondering whether pickup is happening.
Can we switch from our current provider easily?+
Yes. Switching from another valet trash provider is straightforward. We coordinate with your current vendor's end date, handle the resident communication transition, and start service without any gap. Most properties switch over with zero disruption.
