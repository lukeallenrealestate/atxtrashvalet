Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about partnering with ATX Trash Valet for your Austin property.

What is trash valet service? +
Trash valet service is a doorstep pickup service where our team collects trash and recycling directly from each resident's door on a set schedule. It eliminates the need for residents to walk to dumpsters and keeps common areas clean. It's one of the most valued amenities in apartment communities across Austin.
How much does trash valet service cost in Austin? +
Pricing depends on community size, number of units, and pickup frequency. We offer free quotes tailored to your property — no generic pricing tiers. Call us at (254) 718-2567 or fill out our contact form for a custom estimate within 24 hours.
What areas in Austin do you serve? +
We serve apartment communities and condominiums across the greater Austin area, including Downtown, South Austin, North Austin, East Austin, West Austin, The Domain, Mueller, Riverside, West Campus, Round Rock, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, and surrounding neighborhoods.
Do you offer bulk trash pickup? +
Yes. In addition to nightly doorstep valet service, we handle bulk trash pickup for move-outs, property cleanups, and oversized items that don't fit in standard bags. This can be scheduled as a one-time service or added to your regular plan.
How do daily reports work? +
After every service night, property managers and ownership receive a detailed report covering units serviced, any issues encountered (contamination, blocked doors, maintenance concerns), and overall completion status. This gives full visibility into your property's waste management without you needing to check in person.
Can we customize our pickup schedule? +
Absolutely. We build every service plan around your property's specific needs — pickup days, times, and special requirements. Whether you need five nights a week or a custom rotation, we design a schedule that works for your residents and management team.
