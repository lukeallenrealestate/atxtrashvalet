05 — FAQ

Common questions.

What is trash valet service?+
Trash valet service is a doorstep pickup service where our team collects trash and recycling directly from each resident's door on a set schedule. It eliminates the need for residents to walk to dumpsters and keeps common areas clean. It's consistently rated one of the most valued amenities in apartment communities across Austin.
How much does it cost?+
Pricing depends on community size, number of units, and pickup frequency. Every quote is custom-built for your property. Call us at (254) 718-2567 or fill out our form for a free estimate within 24 hours.
What areas do you serve?+
We serve apartment communities across the greater Austin area — Downtown, South Austin, North Austin, East Austin, West Austin, The Domain, Mueller, Riverside, West Campus, Round Rock, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, and surrounding areas.
Do you offer bulk trash pickup?+
Yes. In addition to nightly doorstep valet, we handle bulk trash pickup for move-outs, property cleanups, and oversized items. Schedule one-time or recurring service.
How do daily reports work?+
After every service night, property managers and ownership receive a detailed report — units serviced, issues flagged, contamination notes, and completion status. Delivered automatically every morning.
Can we customize our pickup schedule?+
Absolutely. We build every plan around your property's specific needs — pickup days, times, and special requirements. Whether you need five nights a week or a custom rotation, we design a schedule that works.
