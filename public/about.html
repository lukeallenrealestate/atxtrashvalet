Our Story

We Built This Company
on a Simple Promise

Before starting ATX Trash Valet, we worked with property managers across Austin who were consistently frustrated with their valet trash providers. The complaints were always the same: missed nights, no communication, no accountability, and zero visibility into whether the job was actually getting done.

So we built a company around the opposite approach. Show up every night without fail. Communicate proactively. Send a detailed report after every single service — not when asked, but automatically. And when something goes wrong, own it and fix it the same day.

That's it. No gimmicks, no flashy technology for the sake of it. Just dependable doorstep trash and recycling pickup, executed with discipline and transparency, for apartment communities across Austin that demand better.
