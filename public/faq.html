Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about partnering with ATX Trash Valet for your Austin property.

What is trash valet service?+
Trash valet service is a doorstep pickup service where our team collects trash and recycling directly from each resident's door on a set schedule. It eliminates the need for residents to walk to dumpsters and keeps common areas clean. It's one of the most valued amenities in apartment communities across Austin, reducing hallway odors, dumpster overflow, and resident complaints.
How much does trash valet service cost in Austin?+
Pricing depends on community size, number of units, and pickup frequency. We don't use generic pricing tiers — every quote is custom-built for your property. Call us at (254) 718-2567 or fill out our contact form for a free, no-obligation estimate. Most quotes are delivered within 24 hours.
What areas in Austin do you serve?+
We serve apartment communities and condominiums across the greater Austin area, including Downtown, South Austin, North Austin, East Austin, West Austin, The Domain, Mueller, Riverside, West Campus, Round Rock, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, and surrounding neighborhoods. See our full areas served page for details.
Do you offer bulk trash pickup?+
Yes. In addition to nightly doorstep valet service, we handle bulk trash pickup for move-outs, property cleanups, and oversized items that don't fit in standard bags. This can be scheduled as a one-time service or added to your regular plan.
How do daily reports work?+
After every service night, property managers and ownership receive a detailed report covering units serviced, any issues encountered (contamination, blocked doors, maintenance concerns), and overall completion status. Reports are delivered automatically — you don't need to ask for them. This gives full visibility into your property's waste management.
Can we customize our pickup schedule?+
Absolutely. We build every service plan around your property's specific needs — pickup days, times, and special requirements. Whether you need five nights a week, Sunday through Thursday, or a custom rotation, we design a schedule that works for your residents and management team.
What happens during holidays?+
We operate through most holidays. If a service adjustment is needed, we communicate proactively with your management team well in advance to arrange coverage or make-up service. Your residents won't be left wondering whether pickup is happening.
How quickly can service start?+
In most cases, we can begin service within one week of signing. We'll walk your property, finalize the schedule, provide resident communication materials, and begin nightly pickup. For larger properties, we may need a few extra days for setup.
What if a resident doesn't put out trash?+
No problem at all. Units with no bags out are simply noted in the daily report. Residents are never penalized for not participating on a given night. The service is available every scheduled evening — residents use it when they need it.
Do you handle recycling too?+
Yes, we offer recycling collection as an add-on to your valet plan. We separate recyclables at the door, keeping contamination rates low and diversion rates high. This helps your property meet sustainability goals and gives residents a convenient way to recycle without a separate trip to the bins. Ask about adding recycling when you request your quote.
